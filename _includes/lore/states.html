The Free City of Mythal rises under the shadow of Mt. Rachnyr. It has come far from its humble beginnings as a trading post, gathering a vibrant community of scholars and artisans. In recent years, the leadership of the city has fallen to the esoteric Cult of the Mountain. Little is known about the workings of the cult, except that Mythal has continued to flourish under their rule.

The great city of Achalia was left remarkably intact after the fall of the Ilharic Empire, allowing it to remain a beacon of light and safe haven for refugees in the wake of the collapse. This stability was cemented by a series of militaristic Emperors who seized much of the surrounding lands, forming what they viewed as the natural heir to Ilharim. Over the last century, another wave of expansion and conquest has proceeded up the Sea of Morin. While their eyes have yet to turn inland, the threat of their continued expansion hangs over the other kingdoms in the region.

The Princes of Gilvry are descendants of those warlords and governors who fled the growing might of the Achalian empire, and in the highlands forged their own compact. The people of Gilvry are proud of their history and their freedom. Notionally, all lords and princes serve the court of the Grand Prince, but the common [independence] leaves Gilvry politics frequently more mercurial than that.

The Kingdom of Uwlleth is a land of diverse geography. From the temperate forests rising thorough the Acheron foothills, to the jungles and marshes along the coast, to the fertile riverlands between, life flourishes. Dotted throughout are manors, cultivating a range of crops and spices that dominate the trade in the region.
