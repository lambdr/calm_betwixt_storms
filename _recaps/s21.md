The Embers of the Vale continued their exploration of Inlarut. Contessa and Saskarill returned to the market in search of jewellery and information. They made their way to a tavern and were approached by a dishevelled young man who needed help dealing with a cockatrice – a petrifying birdlike monstrosity. 

Ellywick visited a poorer temple in the city, having an unsatisfactory conversation with an angelic statue and a more satisfying grave sweeping moment with a Cleric of Amarhus. Regill and Jadwiga found an underground tavern, where criminals can talk, gamblers can play, and the brave can try their hand in a fighting ring. Now the party heads to a dinner party at Baiat’s with plans to confront the cockatrice in the morning.
