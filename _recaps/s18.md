The party continued the battle with the mushroom people (who Regill suggested might be myconids) while Contessa took a beauty sit-down. While Ellywick, Saskarill, and Jadwiga slew the guards, Regill shoved another one in a bag. As they continued on through the caverns, they found another mushroom farm, with another group of mushrooms. 

The group bullied one of these mushrooms (with some pictograms from Jadwiga) until one of the myconid farmers led the party to their village center. There, the older myconids deployed their telepathic spores to communicate with the party, expressing an extreme displeasure of the party’s slaughter of their workers and guards. The party eventually recovered the body of Ekin, the deceased farmer, returning their captured youngling, some pachi juice and promising to seal the hole to these caverns.

The Embers returned to Penrith with the body (Contessa and Regill staying far from sight), where a grateful Philine told them that her husband was at rest at last. As they departed, the Reeve left no ambiguity as to the fact that their party members were not welcome in the hamlet. Now, the group has reunited with Iacob and Matias, and continues on the road to Inlarut.
