The party continued their quest to recover the body of the dead villager Ekin. After some difficulty, the group found the tree with seven trunks, and the cave in a rocky outcropping. Inside, they found some mushrooms and a collapsed section of the ground. They descended by rope to a large cavern rich with mushrooms, and identified bloody splotches and a bag of foraged mushrooms.

While they proceeded through the cave network, they began to see signs of construction, small walls, and a farm. Tending the farm was a 1.5 foot tall moving mushroom. When it saw Regill, it poofed out a cloud of spores and ran away. Regill chased after it, into a room with three other mushroom people, and struck it down in front of the others. The mushrooms swarmed Regill and began to attack, sending out more clouds of spores. 

Large mushrooms ran from deeper in the cave and joined the fray. One struck Contessa with directed spores, pacifying her. As she, mellowed out, sits to watch this confusing battle unfold, the rest of the party battles on.
