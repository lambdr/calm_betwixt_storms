The party cleaned off the sewer filth following their unsatisfactory dealing with the thief Beirun. Ellywick returned to the church for an urgent conversation with Mother Zemi.

While the party perused the gambling floor, Jadwiga met some interesting individuals: Ulblyn Moth, a fellow follower of Lady Eresh, who offered to teach her the ways of the Luckbloade, and Amada the Wraith, a prominent member of the Hand – the Thieves’ Guild of Inlarut. Amada – who it seems employed Beirun – returned the money promised plus the miscreants ears. Jadwiga posted a letter to the Kingdom of Uwlleth. 

The party then spent some days honing their skills in light of their recent experience, and return to the College of the Naga to deal with Nuavan Karsk.
