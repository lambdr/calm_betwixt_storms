The Embers of the Vale sought out Baiat, son of Prieten. They visited the Mason’s Guild to learn where he resides, and found him on his way out. Baiat thanked them for delivering a letter from Prieten and invited them to a dinner the following evening, on the equinox.

From there, the party visited the College of the Naga, finding it difficult to get in on their word alone, and eventually showing Lord Ryazan’s letter. A guard led them to Nuavan Karsk, who expressed interest in Matias, but was unwilling to help the party directly until they’d proven themselves and done him a favor.

Disgruntled, the party left the tower, only to be held up by sleek rat, with whom Ellywick began conversing. The rat, named Theo, told her a bit about the college and his master, the purple-eyed wizard Llewellyn. Llewellyn talked a bit with the party and invited them to return to speak with him about their magical blade in the first days of Jaseril. That evening, the group returned to the Silver Hoof, to find Iacob upset that he’d been abandoned for the day. He left, with Matias, taking his son alone to the College.
